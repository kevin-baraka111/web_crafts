KABARAK UNIVERSITY

ABOUT

History

Kabarak University is a premier private chartered institution in Kenya, established in 2000 by the late former President Daniel T. Arap Moi, offering holistic Christian-based education. Located on a 600-acre main campus in Nakuru, it focuses on developing strong character,integrity, and academic excellence , and it officially opened for students in 2002

Location

The Main Campus is situated 20km from Nakuru City along the Nakuru-Eldama Ravine road. A modern Nakuru City Campus operates in the city center for part-time/evening students.
